We Find Businesses That Actually Fit Your Life

Most business plan tools ask you to pick a business. We start with you — your specific limitations, your location, your goals — and find what actually works.

🦽 Mobility Limitations

Businesses that work around mobility challenges — home-based, mobile service, or online businesses that don't require standing all day.

🧠 PTSD & Traumatic Brain Injury

Low-stress environments with flexible schedules. businesses with minimal customer confrontation and manageable sensory demands.

👁️ Vision Impairment

Business models that leverage other senses or compensate with technology. Home-based services, consulting, and digital businesses.

🎯 Chronic Pain or Fatigue

Businesses with predictable schedules and manageable physical demands. Passive income streams and scalable models preferred.
